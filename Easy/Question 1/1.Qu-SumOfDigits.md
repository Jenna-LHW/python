**Question 1: Given a number n, find the sum of its digits.**

Examples:
```
Input: n = 687
Output: 21
Explanation: The sum of its digits are: 6 + 8 + 7 = 21

Input: n = 12
Output: 3
Explanation: The sum of its digits are: 1 + 2 = 3
```
